Welcome to the LongGeneDB!

This is the home page of the ***Long Gene Database (LongGeneDB)***.

A user can enter the name of a long gene (>200 kb) here and will get the gene's evolutionary history, conservation, tissue-specific transcriptome, tissue-specific epigenome, and single cell transcriptome.
